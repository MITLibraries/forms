Thank You

Thank you for contacting the Institute Archives and Special Collections. Staff members will respond to non-emergency questions Monday through Thursday. Questions from the MIT community are given highest priority.

You will receive a copy of your request via email.

(If you don't receive a copy, it's possible that your request didn't go through. In that case, please send a message to Ask Us!)

Please note:

  • Time-sensitive requests will receive our attention five days a week. We will check "Ask the Archives" daily, but please feel free to contact us via telephone (3-5690) or fax (8-7305) with urgent requests.
  • Most of our collections are stored off site and require 24-48 hours to retrieve.
  • Many of our collections have restrictions on access because they are unprocessed, or due to MIT access policies or restrictive donor agreements.

Please address follow-up correspondence to mithistory@mit.edu, rather than to individual staff members, who may be away from the office.
