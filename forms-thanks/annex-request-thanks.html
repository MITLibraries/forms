Annex Request

Thank you! Your request is being forwarded to the MIT Libraries' Library Storage Annex. You may pick it up at the circulation desk in the library you selected. Meanwhile, you will receive confirmation by email.

If you don't receive confirmation, it's possible that your request didn't go through. In that case, please fill out the form again, or contact us.

  • Materials are normally delivered within 24 hours or less.
  • You should receive email notification when your items arrive at the destination library.
  • Anyone may call the destination library to verify that an item has arrived.
