Suggest a Purchase

The MIT Libraries welcome suggestions for the acquisition of materials that support teaching, study, and research at the Institute. Before you make a request for purchase, please check our catalog, Barton, to be sure that the item is not already in the MIT Libraries' collection.

If you need materials immediately that are not available in the MIT Libraries--including journal articles--please use our Interlibrary Borrowing Service.

To submit course reserve and textbook information, please go to tip.mit.edu.
