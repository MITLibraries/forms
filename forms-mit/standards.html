Standards Request Form

Please read the following policies before placing a request. This form is for current members of the MIT community only.

Format / Delivery:

  • We CANNOT email a PDF to you. Contracts for purchasing standards do not allow us to share an electronic copy.
  • We will circulate a PRINT copy of the standard. Please keep this in mind if you are off-campus.

Price:

  • The MIT Libraries will make every effort to obtain the standard you need at no cost to you. But keep in mind we have a limited budget for purchasing standards and they can be expensive.
  • If you need multiple standards or standards with multiple parts, you may be asked to prioritize.

Time:

  • Typical delivery time will be 7-10 days. Some cases may take longer.
  • Delivery time will vary based on standards vendor practices and policies.
