Special Borrower Card Application

If your work requires extensive use of resources not available at MIT, you may qualify for access for some locations of the Harvard College Library. See more details about privileges at Harvard's collections.

Borrowing privileges Card valid from
Faculty and Research Staff September 1 – August 31

For summer access, students may reapply for a Special Borrower Card, valid for one year, beginning June 1. (The Harvard summer letter is no longer needed.)
