MIT Open Access Publishing Fund

Form to request support from the Open Access Article Publication Subvention Fund (OAAPSF)

This form should used by MIT faculty authors to request funding for open access publication fees from eligible journals.

">

The request will be reviewed for eligibility and the author informed whether it has been approved.

If the request is approved, the author will need to forward proof of payment for the publication charges to Ellen Duranceau, Program Manager, Scholarly Publishing & Licensing, or mail it to Room 14S-216.

Payment will be made directly to the journal on the author's behalf.

Required fields are followed by *.
