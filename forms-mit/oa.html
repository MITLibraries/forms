MIT Open Access Publishing Fund

Form to request support from the Open Access Article Publication Subvention Fund (OAAPSF)

This form should be used by MIT affiliates to request funding for open access publication fees from eligible journals. The request will be reviewed for eligibility and the author informed whether it has been approved. If the request is approved, the author will need to forward proof of payment for the publication charges to Katharine Dunn, Scholarly Communication Librarian. Payment will be reimbursed to an MIT account.
